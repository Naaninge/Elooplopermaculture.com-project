About Us

The Eloolo Permaculture Initiative is a group of young environmentalist and activists engaged in education, research, consultation, implementation of food systems and associated Permaculture projects.

We strive to improve human ecosystems to become healthy and abundant, guided by our ethics: Care for the people, care for the earth and fair share!
