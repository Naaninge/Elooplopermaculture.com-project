Clean Travel

The Clean Travel program is Namibia's first carbon
offsetting offer. By donating a tree/s you help offset
your carbon use/emissions as well as help educate
children and communities about the value of growing
food and trees.

Klippdachs Kaserne

Eloolo Permaculture Initiative has partnered up with
the National Museum of Namibia and is currently
renovating the Klippdachs Kaserne, also previously
known as the 'Camel Stables'.

Read More

Farm Okukuna

Farm Okukuna is a visionary new project to enhance
food and nutrition security in the informal settlements
of Windhoek. The farm act as the centre for a number
of programmes, including growing food, marketing it
and engaging the community on healthy nutrition.
